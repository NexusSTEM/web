Our Mission


We are a fiscally sponsored 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization and at NexusSTEM, our mission is to ignite a passion for technology and STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) among students and communities. We are dedicated to making STEM accessible, engaging, and fun through a variety of programs and resources.

We believe that technology and STEM education have the power to transform lives and open doors to limitless opportunities. That‚Äôs why we offer:

  • Hands-On Workshops: Interactive sessions that bring STEM concepts to life and help students develop practical skills.
  • Exciting Events: Engaging gatherings that foster collaboration, innovation, and learning in technology and STEM fields.
  • Informative Newsletters: Regular updates and insights to keep you informed about the latest trends, opportunities, and resources in STEM.
  • Free Magazine: A resourceful publication designed specifically for middle school, high school, and college students to inspire and educate them about STEM topics.
  • Resources and Incentives: Free tools and rewards for students who build and ship their projects, providing them with practical experience and recognition for their achievements.

Our goal is to empower the next generation of innovators and problem solvers by teaching them how to harness the power of technology and STEM to achieve their dreams and make a positive impact in the world.

Join us at NexusSTEM as we explore, create, and advance the future of technology and STEM together!
